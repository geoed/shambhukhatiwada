Teachings

  • Current Practice in Social Studies|Sost. Ed. 722
    M. Phil.(Second Year)
    Tribhuvan University, Faculty of Education (FoE) has introduced the M. Phil. Programme based courses in Social Studies education. This course is designed to provide the students with knowledge of Current Practices in Social Studies. This course aims to help students acquire an understanding of current practices in social studies with relation to philosophy and theoretical grounds linking with modernist and post-modernist perspectives. It also helped students acquire knowledge of philosophy and theories regarding to contemporary society such as gender, caste/ethnicity and inclusions along with its pedagogical implication at various levels in the context of Nepal.
    [PDF]

  • Fundamentals of Social Studies|Sost. Ed. 4185
    PGD Ed.(First Year)
    This course is designed for the students who specialize in Post Graduate Diploma in Education (PGD Ed.) in Social Studies Education. This course is designed to orient to the theoretical knowledge of Social Studies Education. Its aim is to provide them a comprehensive knowledge of Social Studies Education. The contents of this course have been divided into five units. These units give emphasis on the meaning and the basic concept of social studies. After completing this course the students will be aquatinted with the fundamental base of the Social Studies Education System.
    [PDF]
